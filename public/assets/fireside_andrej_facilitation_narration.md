# Fireside Recap Narration: Andrej Berlin / AI Facilitation

## Intro
Fireside recap: Andrej Berlin on AI-enabled facilitation, sensemaking, attention scarcity, Deep Work, and human curation.

## AI as facilitation layer
Andrej framed AI as a facilitation layer: useful for turning many people's inputs into shared language that participants recognize as their own.

## AI-assisted sensemaking
The group explored AI-assisted sensemaking: extracting signal from interviews, surveys, profiles, Discord, and Telegram into summaries, dashboards, recommendations, and collaborator matches.

## Attention scarcity
As AI makes it easier to build and generate more, the harder problem becomes choosing what deserves focus.

## Renewed Deep Work
Andrej described a renewed Deep Work direction: a network of experts, founders, residencies, product and design support, AI-enabled research, prototyping, and collective learning.

## Taste and curation
The session kept returning to human taste and curation. AI can expand options, but someone still needs to provide direction, judgment, and a point of view.
