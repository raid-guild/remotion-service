Voice direction: Queen Raida. Calm signal. Occult operator. Mysterious, sharp, more invocation than explainer.

The old coordination machines had faults. Still, something inside them kept humming.

## Scene 2 - Watchtower

Now the treasury dark opens. Shared vaults. Proposal gates. Moloch-shaped exits. The next machine wants agents at the edge, humans at the gate, and receipts in the open.
