# Fireside Recap Narration: CJ Miller / AI Copywriting

## Intro
Fireside recap: CJ Miller on AI copywriting, human voice, GEO, content tells, and why trust may move back offline.

## AI gutted basic copywriting
CJ estimates demand for standalone copywriting dropped around ninety percent after ChatGPT, forcing agencies to reposition around strategy, expertise, and quality.

## Human voice is the premium
Generic AI content is everywhere. CJ argues the real value now comes from lived experience, expert perspective, and writing that does not feel over-polished.

## GEO is mostly good SEO
AI search optimization is real as a market category, but the fundamentals still matter: clear site structure, topical authority, backlinks, and readable service pages.

## AI content has tells
The group dug into common AI writing patterns: em dash overload, not X but Y framing, polished sameness, and forced punchy sentence fragments.

## Trust moves back offline
As AI-generated outreach floods feeds and inboxes, CJ expects conferences, in-person meetings, and relationship-driven channels to become more valuable again.
