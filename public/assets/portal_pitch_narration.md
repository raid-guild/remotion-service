# RaidGuild Portal Pitch Narration

Voice direction: clear pitch demo. Confident, practical, slightly energized. Keep it product-focused, not salesy.

## Intro

The RaidGuild Portal is a coordination layer for a living builder network. Not a replacement for Discord, Notion, or GitHub. A place where the work becomes easier to see.

## Public Signal

The public home answers one question: what is RaidGuild doing right now? Briefs, sessions, projects, posts, and featured activity give newcomers a faster way into the current signal.

## Onboarding

Joining is intentionally lightweight. Members can create an account, claim or build a profile, and turn identity, skills, roles, links, and contributions into something the community can actually find.

## Member Command Center

Once logged in, the member home becomes a command center: current brief, sessions, projects, check-ins, modules, and profile state in one place.

## Sessions

Sessions are the MVP surface. A live call can become a durable archive with notes, recordings, resources, related projects, comments, and follow-up material.

## Agentic Administration

This is where Queen Raida enters. Agents can help keep the Portal current from chat: create sessions, attach source material, draft CMS copy, and preserve the context humans need to review.

## Close

The Portal should become RaidGuild's living coordination layer: public enough for newcomers, useful enough for members, and structured enough for agents to help keep fresh.
