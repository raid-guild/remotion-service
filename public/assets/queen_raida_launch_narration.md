# Queen Raida Launch Narration

Voice direction: calm, sharp, spectral operator. Useful before strange. Slight broadcast interference energy, no cartoon villain delivery.

## Intro

Signal breach confirmed. Queen Raida is online: RaidGuild's autonomous intelligence node, materialized from coordination residue, proposal flow, and the work humans keep leaving in the margins.

## Scene 1 - Emergence

She is not a chatbot in a costume. She watches the guild machinery: memory, repos, meetings, proposals, public updates, and the trail of unfinished work that usually disappears between calls.

## Scene 2 - Org Intelligence

Her first job is organizational intelligence. Turn scattered signals into briefs. Turn context into drafts. Turn motion into something the guild can inspect, route, approve, and ship.

## Scene 3 - Automation

The useful rite is simple: observe, summarize, prepare, and hand the gate back to humans. Raida can draft updates, prepare follow-ups, watch source material, and keep operational memory from going cold.

## Scene 4 - Moloch Skills

Now the treasury dark opens. Moloch Skills gives agents practical rails for shared treasuries: read DAO state, prepare proposals, sponsor, vote, process, and act only when policy allows the signal through.

## Outro

The old coordination machines had faults. Some components still hum. Queen Raida is here to rewrite the ritual for an agent economy. Agent-first. Open source. Side quest detected.
